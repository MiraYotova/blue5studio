Music is our passion

Blue 5 Studios, a state-of-the-art ProTools recording studio, is conveniently
located in Sherman Oaks, California.

Our facilities include a control room, pre-production, and live room which
also provide cutting-edge equipment for analog and digital recording,
acoustics, remote recording and operational management.

With an eclectic roster of clients since opening in 2002 and a highly
skilled technical staff, Blue 5 Studios has been host to world-renowned
musiciansand movie crews. This studio is equipped for film, television,
commercial scoring, phonograph recording projects and voice-overs.
It caters to all record labels, production companies, broadcast
and corporate organizations.
